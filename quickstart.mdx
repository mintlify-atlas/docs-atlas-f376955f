title: "Quick start"
description: "Log in to MAPRISER ATOTO and generate your first official document."
---

<Warning>
  Your session closes automatically after **10 minutes of inactivity**. A toast notification warns you one minute before the session ends. Any unsaved progress in an open form will be lost. Save your work or complete your document before stepping away.
</Warning>

Follow these steps to go from your first login to a downloaded PDF document.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the application">
    Open the MAPRISER ATOTO URL in your browser. The application works best on desktop. Contact the Transparencia e Informática area for the current deployment URL if you do not have it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in with your institutional credentials">
    Enter your institutional email address (for example, `nombre@atotonilcodetula.gob.mx`) and your password, then click **Iniciar sesión**.

    If you see an error that says "Usuario inactivo", your account has been disabled. Contact your administrator at `transparencia-informatica@atotonilcodetula.gob.mx`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept the privacy notice (first login only)">
    On your first login, the **Aviso de Privacidad Integral** dialog opens automatically and cannot be dismissed. Read through all sections using the navigation panel on the left. At the bottom, check the box labeled **He leído y acepto el Aviso de Privacidad** and click **Aceptar y continuar**.

    This step is required only once. If you have already accepted the notice in a previous session, you go directly to the password change screen or the dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your personal password (first login only)">
    After accepting the privacy notice, the **Establece tu contraseña** screen appears. Choose a password of at least 6 characters, confirm it, and click **Guardar contraseña y continuar**.

    Your password must match in both fields. A strength indicator shows whether the password is too short, acceptable, or strong. This step is performed only once at account activation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View the dashboard and browse available machotes">
    After logging in you land on the main dashboard — **Machotes Disponibles**. The page shows all document templates available for your assigned area, grouped by department. Use the search bar to filter templates by name, area, or description.

    Administrators see machotes from all nine areas. Standard users see only the machotes for their assigned area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a machote and complete the required fields">
    Click the template card you want to use. The document editor opens and shows the dynamic fields defined in that machote. Fill in every required field — each field corresponds to a `[VARIABLE]` placeholder in the template.

    <Info>
      Field labels are defined by the area head or administrator who created the machote. If a field label is unclear, contact your area head for guidance.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview the document with the institutional letterhead">
    Once the fields are filled, the preview panel renders the document with the official letterhead of your area. Review the output carefully before proceeding — the letterhead image, your input values, and the document layout are all visible at this stage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download as PDF or print">
    Click **Descargar PDF** to save the document to your device, or click **Imprimir** to send it directly to a connected printer. The document is recorded in the system's history log with your name, area, timestamp, and the machote used.
  </Step>
</Steps>
