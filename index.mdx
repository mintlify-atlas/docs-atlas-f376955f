## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Log in with your institutional email and password. On first login you will be prompted to accept the privacy notice and set a new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template">
    From the dashboard, browse the machotes available for your area. Each template is pre-configured with the fields you need to fill in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the fields">
    Complete the dynamic fields in the form. Required fields are marked — the system extracts these automatically from the `[VARIABLE]` placeholders in the template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview, download, or print">
    Review the rendered document with the institutional letterhead applied. Download as PDF or send directly to the printer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
